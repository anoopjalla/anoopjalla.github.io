Caravan HackGT8
Explore the World

During HackGT9 in 2021, a group of other like-minded individuals and I came up with an idea. This idea would enable people from across the world to meet new people. Not only, would it enable people to meet new people, but they would be able to travel the world with them. The concept of Caravan is that it enables people to pick a place they wish to visit. Then, the app gives them options of others with similar interests and personality traits that wish to travel to the same place. The app facilitates the process of matching people with others who also wish to travel to new places.

This is an ios iOS app that was developed using Swift, JavaScript, and Xcode. We designed multiple visually appealing pages using Figma. A backend script was developed for users to be able to choose from over 20+ different places to travel and over 100+ people to travel with.
