Campus Event Management Website
CS 2340- Objects and Design

With a group of other like-minded individuals, we developed a campus events management website facilitating seamless RSVP, real-time event tracking, and interactive map-based visualization of campus events for students and administrators. We utilized agile workflow across a 5 month period in order to ensure iterative development and efficient project milestones through biweekly sprints in order to complete the task at hand.

We built an intuitive UI to convey information in an effective way for students to reference using an extensive tech stack including React, JavaScript, and node.js with a backend in Java. For organizational purposes, we diagrammed domain models and applicatoin architecture with system diagrams to facilitate better workflow.
